- Our Story -

We’re a small but nimble family-run take out and delivery pizza shop in Edmonton, Alberta. When we’re not scouring the globe (okay, the prairies) for the freshest, ripest and boldest ingredients, we’re crafting perfect pizzas for you and your family to enjoy. From our artisan brick-fired pizzas, to housemade-made pastas, to our decadent cannolis, everything at Piece on Earth is made with love.

After watching his parents run a very successful pizza business in Italy, Doug Cleveland learned how to make people happy, one topping at a time. Opening the doors to Piece on Earth in 2019, Doug has managed to evolve the fresh, traditional Greek-style pizza his family perfected back in the mid 80s to a more modern and convenient comfort food for today’s family on the go.

- Farm to Table -

Piece on Earth's pizzas are made with only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients like mozzarella, roma tomatoes, made-from-scratch sauce and even features premium toppings like Sorriso Pepperoni, salami from Acme Meat Market, and a healthy flax crust. You feel good about ordering it. You feel good about eating it. And you feel even better knowing we're always ready to make you more. Our drink selection is also sourced by local Alberta breweries, wineries, and distilleries.
